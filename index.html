Should self-driving cars be legalized at the federal level?

Currently, twenty-two states have either passed legislation related to autonomous vehicles or adopted regulations through a governor's executive order. Traditionally, the federal government has regulated the vehicle itself, while states have regulated driver behavior. Should the federal government enact legislation to legalize self-driving cars?

Should American farm subsisidies be reduced by 40%?

American farm subsidies amount to $20 billion a year, collected from federal tax revenue. In 2014, a bill was passed abolishing direct payments based on land ownership. Instead, farmers now get more subsidised insurance, and new payments which are linked to past crop prices and productivity. Should additional legislation be passed to reduce the total amount spent on farm subsidies by 40%?

Should a cap of 25,000 refugees to be accepted in 2018 through the US Refugee Admissions Programme be permanently established?

On 27 January President Donald Trump signed an executive order, part of which introduced a cap of 50,000 refugees to be accepted in 2017. Should there be a cap of 25,000 refugees set for 2018?
